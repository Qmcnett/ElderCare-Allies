Bridging Generation, Creating Friendships

Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive environment for seniors to connect with others.

Many elderly individuals, especially those living alone or with limited social connections, face challenges in maintaining meaningful companionship. In numerous communities, the prevalence of social isolation among seniors has become a pressing concern. Here at ElderCare Allies, we aim to mitigate these issues by fostering intergenerational relationships, connecting volunteers with seniors, and creating a supportive community that enhances the overall well-being of the elderly population. We seek to combat the adverse effects of social isolation, promoting a more fulfilling and connected life for seniors.


Increased risk from loneliness

CDC Statistics and Findings

  • Loneliness among heart failure patients is associated with a nearly 4 times increased risk of death, as well as a 68% increased risk of hospitalization. Additionally, these patients face a 57% increased risk of emergency department visits.
  • Social isolation or loneliness, leading to inadequate social connections, contributes to a 29% higher risk of heart disease.
  • Social isolation or loneliness, lacking sufficient social support, face a 32% increased risk of stroke.
  • Social isolation is linked to about a 50% increased risk of dementia.

How Do We Strive To Make An Impact On Seniors?

Addressing Health Concerns Linked to Social Isolation

Here at ElderCare Allies we are dedicated to tackling the negative consequences of prolonged social isolation, which have been linked to various health issues such as mental health problems, cognitive decline, and an increased risk of chronic diseases. Our goal is to encourage social connections and foster relationships between generations to create a supportive community environment that promotes the overall health and well-being of seniors. Through our programs and initiatives, we aim to minimize the long-term effects of social isolation and provide valuable assistance to individuals and healthcare systems.

Enhancing Seniors' Quality of Life Through Social Connection

Our mission is to lessening the negative impact of prolonged isolation on seniors. We understand that isolation can severely affect their quality of life, which is why we focus on creating opportunities for social interaction and meaningful connections. We strive to promote community engagement and support networks to improve seniors' overall well-being and address the long-lasting effects of isolation. We emphasize the importance of early intervention in overcoming these challenges.


Family Well-being

We believe in promoting the well-being of seniors and their families. We understand that loneliness can have a profound and lasting impact on both seniors and their support networks. That's why we strive to bring families together by fostering intergenerational connections and providing support to seniors who lack social connections. By alleviating the burden on caregivers and strengthening family bonds, we can create a healthier and more fulfilling environment for everyone.
